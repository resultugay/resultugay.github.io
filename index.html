Resul Tugay

I'm a third year PhD student in the LFCS at School of Informatics, The University of Edinburgh supervised by Prof. Wenfei Fan. I'm working on entity representation and learning on both relational and graph-structured data. I develop distributed and parallel systems to manage large data.

I am interested in reasoning and representation of entities. Also, l love camping and nature but, still draw trees upside-down!
