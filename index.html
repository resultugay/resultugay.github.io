Resul Tugay

I am a lecturer at Gazi University. If you want to work on ML, NLP or Recommender Systems and understand this and this and able to code them, please contact me.

I obtained my PhD under supervision of Prof. Wenfei Fan at the The University of Edinburgh in the School of Informatics. I obtained the MSc in Computer Science from the Faculty of Computer and Informatics Engineering, Istanbul Technical University, working with Prof. Sule Gunduz Oguducu. My undergrad was also in computer engineering from Karadeniz Technical University. I'm working on entity representation and learning on both relational and graph-structured data. I also work on Large Language Models.

I am interested in ML/DL and NLP in general. I have experience on Recommender Systems and Ranking. Also, l love camping and nature but, still draw trees upside-down!
